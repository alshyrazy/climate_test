About Us

Ignite Your Voice, Spark Change: YouthClimate Connect

The climate crisis whispers warnings, but youth roar solutions. YouthClimate Connect is your launchpad to amplify your voice, connect with fellow changemakers, and ignite real-world action. Are you ready to be the spark that sets the world ablaze with positive change?

OUR Supporters

YCC was founded by a group of alumni from an Externship program developed by Paragon One in collaboration with The Nature Conservancy and The National Geographic Society. Through this program, we gained valuable knowledge and real world experience in tackling environmental challenges. We decided to use our skills and passion to create YCC, a platform that empowers youth to take action for the climate.

Our Story

We started as a group of passionate young individuals who wanted to make a difference in the face of the urgent climate crisis. We realized that there was a lack of climate education, awareness, and engagement among youth, especially in the global south, where climate issues are particularly severe and pressing. We also noticed that there were many barriers and challenges that prevented youth from accessing information, resources, and opportunities to participate in climate action. We decided to create a platform that would address these gaps and empower youth to take action on the climate crisis.

We launched our platform in 2023, with the aim of reaching and engaging youth from all over the world, with a primary focus on vulnerable regions in the global south. We partnered with various international organizations, experts, and influencers to provide high-quality and relevant climate education resources, gamified learning experiences, community collaboration spaces, and real-world climate project opportunities. We also ensured that our platform was accessible, inclusive, and sustainable, catering to the diverse needs and backgrounds of our users.

Our Vision

To lead a world where youth, especially in vulnerable regions, are at the forefront of climate action, creating a sustainable future. We envision an empowered community of informed and diverse young leaders who collaborate globally to drive transformative climate solutions and foster sustainability.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, innovative, and inclusive tools for youth to become climate champions. We aim to educate, connect, and inspire young leaders through a dynamic online platform. By doing so, we empower them to initiate real-world climate projects, contributing to a sustainable and resilient future.

OUR Programs

Youth and Climate Change Startup Program (YCCSP)

YCCSP is a two-session online initiative that aims to educate and empower young people to take action against climate change. The program covers topics such as the vision and impact of climate change on youth, climate justice in the Global South, and skills to tackle environmental challenges. Participants who attend both sessions will receive a certificate of recognition and join a network of climate activists and changemakers. The program is free and open to everyone.

The Annual YouthClimate Connect Summit

The YouthClimate Connect Summit is a flagship event of the YouthClimate Connect initiative. This annual global summit is dedicated to empowering and engaging youth worldwide in climate action. It serves as a platform where young leaders from diverse backgrounds come together to showcase innovative climate projects, solutions, and ideas while connecting with international organizations, experts, and influencers

FAQs

Find answers to frequently asked questions about YCC and how to get involved in climate action initiatives.

What is YouthClimate Connect (YCC)?

YCC is a youth-led global initiative that aims to empower and mobilize youth to take meaningful action in response to the pressing climate crisis. YCC provides a comprehensive online platform that offers climate education, gamified learning, community collaboration, inclusivity, and sustainability for young leaders, especially in the global south.

Why is YCC focused on the global south?

YCC recognizes that climate injustice in the global south is a critical and pressing issue that requires immediate attention and action. The global south faces disproportionate impacts of climate change, such as rising temperatures, droughts, floods, storms, and sea level rise, which threaten the lives and livelihoods of millions of people, especially the youth. YCC aims to address the gap in climate awareness, access to information, and engagement opportunities for youth in these regions, and to empower them to become climate champions.

How does YCC educate youth about climate change?

YCC offers engaging, multilingual climate education resources, including videos and courses, to equip youth with the knowledge and skills needed to address climate challenges effectively. YCC also incorporates gamification elements to make climate education interactive and enjoyable, fostering greater engagement and understanding.
