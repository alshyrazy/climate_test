Help Youth Fight The Climate Crisis

Image by jcomp on Freepik

YCC supports youth-led climate action, empowering young people everywhere, for a better future.

That means a donation to YouthClimate Connect could help a young leader in Khartoum launch a green entrepreneurship project, or fund a climate education workshop for a schoolgirl in Dhaka. Or, your gift could sponsor a youth delegate to attend the COP28 summit, and advocate for climate justice on the global stage. No matter who you are, we believe that youth have the potential to make a difference in the climate crisis. When you give a gift to YouthClimate Connect, you help us support youth initiatives that are innovative and impactful.
